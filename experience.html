As for working experience, I don't have one.






Starting on 25th October 2023, for the purpose of assignment, I decided to sell Mango Chicken with capital of RM150.00 at the beginning. I sold the Mango Chicken for RM6.00 at UiTM Campus Kota Bharu Student Corner. At first, I sell it just for fun for the purpose to gain assignment marks. But whenever I slowly learnt on how to calculate for the costing and closing sales, unfortunately, the sales of Mango Chicken didn't achieve the target. I cried. It is because I put a lot of efforts to make the sales.





But, I'm not giving up! I keep looking for solution to cover the capital has spent. After two days, with the help of Allah SWT, one of my friends suggesting on selling Begedil Ayam. Thought twice on the recommendation given. Hmmm. And then, I give it a try on it, again! Waking up at 2.30am, sometimes at 1.00am just for preparing the ingredients and packing Begedil Ayam for the customers on my own. In the mean time, I followed up the feedbacks from the customers. And I keep improving from time-to-time and day-by-day. Being a food seller is not easy. Yes, I admit that as a seller, I want to achieve the target or in another word, profit! However, always and always putting a thought on the customer first. Secure the products with quality, then identity of the foods will come after. I don't have any experience in selling the foods unless helping my parents at night market. To sum up the stories, within 49 days, I managed to achieve the profit of Begedil Ayam with RM1,312.50. The only word that I uttered at that time is "Alhamdulillah". My efforts has paid off.





InsyaAllah, during semester break, I will continue selling Begedil Ayam and Tauhu Berlauk as for my side income. Tauhu Berlauk with Sambal Kicap. Nyum Nyum! Stay tuned, okay! May Allah ease everything.
